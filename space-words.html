Help & Support
✕
How do I form words? ›
Drag your finger across the letter orb to connect letters and form a word. Release to submit. The path line shows your current selection.
What are bonus words? ›
Bonus words aren't required to finish the level, but finding them earns extra coins and XP. They're shown separately from required words.
How does the daily challenge work? ›
A new puzzle unlocks every day at midnight. Complete it for 3× XP and special rewards. Daily puzzles reset every 24 hours.
What are streaks? ›
Play at least once per day to grow your streak. Reach Day 7 for a gem reward. Missing a day resets your streak back to zero.
How do I earn coins? ›
Coins drop for completing levels, finding bonus words, and finishing daily quests. Use them to buy hints and power-ups in the game.
How do I unlock new planets? ›
Complete all required words on the current planet to unlock the next one. Earn stars by finding more bonus words and completing challenges.
